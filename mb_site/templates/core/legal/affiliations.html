No Fake or Forced Partnerships Policy

Authentic Partnerships Only

MarketBrainers maintains strict policies against fake endorsements, forced affiliations, and deceptive partnerships.

1. Authentic Partnership Philosophy

We believe in building genuine, mutually beneficial relationships with platforms, influencers, and partners. Our reputation is built on authenticity and transparency.

2. No Fake Endorsements

We strictly prohibit:

  • Fake platform listings or rankings
  • Artificial influencer endorsements
  • Paid reviews presented as organic
  • Fabricated media coverage
  • False celebrity or expert affiliations

3. No Forced Partnerships

We do not engage in:

  • Coercive partnership arrangements
  • Undisclosed paid placements
  • Platform manipulation for rankings
  • Artificial trend creation
  • Deceptive community engagement

4. Platform Independence

We maintain independence from all platforms we work with. Campaign placement decisions are based on:

  • Target audience alignment
  • Campaign objectives
  • Performance data and analytics
  • Client-specific requirements
  • Platform credibility and reach

5. Transparent Campaign Execution

All campaigns are executed with full transparency:

  • Clear identification of paid placements
  • Honest performance reporting
  • Accurate platform representation
  • Genuine community engagement
  • Authentic influencer collaborations

6. Verification Processes

We implement rigorous verification:

  • Platform authentication and validation
  • Influencer credibility checks
  • Media outlet verification
  • Performance metric auditing
  • Regular compliance reviews

7. Client Education

We educate clients on:

  • Realistic campaign expectations
  • Authentic marketing practices
  • Long-term brand building
  • Sustainable growth strategies
  • Ethical marketing standards

8. Reporting Violations

If you suspect any violation of this policy:

  • Contact us immediately through official channels
  • Provide specific details and evidence
  • Allow for investigation and resolution
  • We take all reports seriously and investigate promptly

9. Consequences of Violations

Violations of this policy may result in:

  • Immediate service termination
  • Legal action where applicable
  • Platform blacklisting
  • Industry notification
  • Full transparency in resolution

Our Commitment

MarketBrainers is committed to maintaining the highest standards of integrity, authenticity, and transparency in all our partnerships and campaign executions.
