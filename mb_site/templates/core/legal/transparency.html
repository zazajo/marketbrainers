Transparency Statement

Our Commitment to Transparency

At MarketBrainers, we believe in complete transparency in our operations, partnerships, and campaign execution. This document outlines our transparency principles and practices.

1. Campaign Reporting

  • Real-time campaign performance dashboards
  • Detailed platform-by-platform reporting
  • Monthly comprehensive performance reviews
  • Transparent communication of challenges and solutions

2. Partnership Disclosure

  • Clear identification of all platform partners
  • No hidden or undisclosed affiliations
  • Transparent fee structures and commission models
  • Independent platform selection based on campaign needs

3. Data Handling

  • Secure client data protection protocols
  • No unauthorized data sharing
  • GDPR and data privacy compliance
  • Transparent data usage policies

4. Campaign Coordination

  • Clear communication channels
  • Regular status updates
  • Transparent campaign adjustments
  • Open discussion of strategy changes

5. Platform Balance

We maintain platform independence and do not favor any specific platforms. Campaign placement decisions are based on:

  • Target audience alignment
  • Campaign objectives
  • Performance data and analytics
  • Client-specific requirements

Note: All campaign coordination is handled privately, but our processes remain transparent to our clients.
